Homemade Pierogies

Pierogies, boiled dumplings, are very flexible and can be stuffed with a number of savory or sweet fillings, including potato and cheese.

Ingriedients

  • Flour
  • Spices and seasoning(salt and pepper)
  • Large egg
  • Potatoes
  • Quark
  • Sour Cream

Instructions

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade pierogies:

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Make the filling.
  3. Fill the pierogi.
  4. Boil pierogi.
  5. Put sour cream on them.
